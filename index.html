Caffe Latte, A Best Seller

₹500

A Caffè Latte is a popular espresso-based coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam. It has a smooth, creamy texture and a mild coffee flavor, making it a favorite for those who enjoy a balanced taste between coffee and milk. The term "latte" comes from the Italian word for milk, emphasizing its creamy nature. It can be customized with flavors like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut.

Strawberry mocha, A Best Seller

₹500

A Strawberry Mocha is a rich and indulgent coffee drink that blends the bold taste of espresso with the sweetness of chocolate and the fruity essence of strawberries. It features a smooth combination of steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and strawberry flavoring, creating a creamy and flavorful beverage. Often topped with whipped cream and drizzled with chocolate or strawberry sauce, this drink offers a perfect balance of bitterness, sweetness, and fruitiness, making it a unique and refreshing twist on the classic mocha.

Doppio espresso, A Best Seller

₹500

Doppio Espresso is a double shot of espresso, made by pulling two shots of espresso from finely ground coffee using an espresso machine. It has a strong, bold flavor with a rich crema on top and a concentrated caffeine kick. With no added milk or sugar, a Doppio highlights the deep, intense coffee notes, making it a favorite for espresso lovers who enjoy a pure and robust coffee experience.

Matcha latte macchiato, A Best Seller

₹500

Matcha Latte Macchiato is a layered tea-based beverage that combines the earthy richness of matcha with creamy steamed milk and a light frothy top. It features a base of warm, frothed milk, followed by a carefully poured shot of matcha green tea, creating a beautiful layered effect. The drink balances the natural bitterness of matcha with the sweetness of milk, offering a smooth, slightly vegetal flavor with a hint of sweetness. Often topped with foam or a drizzle of honey, it's a visually appealing and refreshing alternative to traditional coffee-based macchiatos.
